Shengfei "Gabriel" Yin, Ph.D.

Operations Research Researcher, Deep Learning Learner, Cat Fanatic

  • Senior Software Engineer - Lead Professional at GE Vernova, Inc.
  • Past Senior Optimization Engineer at Ascend Analytics, LLC in 2022-2024.
  • Past Postdoc at the Lawrence Berkeley Lab in 2021-2022.
  • Past Researcher at Midcontinent Independent System Operator, Inc. in 2020-2021.
  • Past Research Intern at PSEC of National Renewable Energy Laboratory in 2019.

I received the Ph.D. degree from Southern Methodist University, Dallas, TX, in 2021, the M.S. degree from Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago, IL, in 2017, and the B. Eng. degree from Hunan University, Changsha, China, in 2016, all majoring in Electrical Engineering. My Ph.D. advisor is Prof. Jianhui Wang, IEEE Fellow. My Master advisor is Prof. Zuyi Li, IEEE Fellow.

I serve as Reviewer for many reputable journals, e.g., AE, TSG, TPWRS, TSTE, etc. I love deep-diving in Operations Research, Artificial Intelligence and their applications in Energy Market. Though I have settled in the U.S. energy industry, I still hold tremendous passion for academic research, so I look forward to connecting with fellow researchers!
